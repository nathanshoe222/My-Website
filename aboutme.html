About Me

My name is Nathan Schuh. I'm a media studies major and history minor at Queens College. I grew up in upstate New York before moving to Queens for school. My hobbies include music (I play guitar, bass, and drums), hanging out with my roommates and my community, and critiquing YouTube videos. I also work part-time as a driving instructor, which I have been doing for a year now.
